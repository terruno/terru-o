Terruño Único

Terruño,… ? What’s that!?

‘Terroir’ in French… There’s no such word that presents the same significance in English nor in Flemish-Dutch;… Except,… the same word that we adopted: ‘Terroir’

But we understand the meaning instinctively as the whole of elements that represents a single geographical place (Soil, Micro-Biology, yeasts, Plant-variaties, Climate,… ) together with the policy of the producer,…

What it means FOR US:

All these elements together that you can notice, smell, taste, experience! in one single glass of wine: personality, originality, recognizability… And once you get to know each other better, the feeling of ‘Déjà Vu’, something you cannot describe in words, but what you recognize is there… as a good friend… as home.

Terruño Único stands for our eternal search for these local personalities against mediocrity and commercial mainstream. Where’s no need of use for chemicals to keep there crops and plantations alive and healthy. Where usually we meet small-scaled management and very personal approach, a direct contact with owners and growers.

To establish a symbiosis between every piece of the chain:

From the Soil…

into a glass of wine, a sprinkle of olive oil or a gastronomical ingredient…

…and back…
