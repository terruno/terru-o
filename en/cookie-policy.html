Cookie Policy

This website uses only technical cookies necessary for its proper functioning. It does not use analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking cookies.

What are cookies?

A cookie is a small file stored in the user's browser when visiting certain web pages. Its purpose can vary, such as saving browsing preferences or improving the user experience.

Cookies used

  • cookieconsent_status: Stores the user's consent for accepting cookies. Duration: 1 year. Type: technical.

How to disable cookies

Users can configure their browser to block or delete cookies. This may affect the proper functioning of the website.
