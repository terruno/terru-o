BODEGAS EMILIO VALERIO | Laderas de Montejurra

Emilio Valerio - Laderas de Montejurra is a family-run winery known for its commitment to preserving the biodiversity of the Montejurra valleys, practising biodynamic agriculture, and producing high-quality wines on the southern slopes of Montejurra, across more than 50 small plots in the Tierra Estella subzone.

In addition to its renowned wines, Emilio Valerio also produces an exclusive extra virgin olive oil. This is a premium-grade oil, obtained directly from olives and solely through mechanical means, using cold extraction to preserve its flavour and natural properties to the fullest.

The oil is made entirely from Arróniz olives, a native variety that gives it a distinctive character and a strong sense of place. Production is strictly limited, with a maximum of 3,000 half-litre bottles per harvest. It has an exceptionally low acidity level of just 0.2%, a clear indicator of the extraordinary quality and care invested throughout the entire production process.
