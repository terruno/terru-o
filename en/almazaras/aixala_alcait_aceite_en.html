CELLER AIXALÁ I ALCAIT

The winery, located in the heart of the DOQ Priorat (Torroja del Priorat), has been producing wines since 2005 and organic wines since 2008—all made exclusively from grapes grown in its own vineyards.

The Priorat region is known for its ability to slow the passage of time, honouring nature, tradition, and the hard work of its people. Aixalà i Alcait seeks to express the full personality of this land, and the production from their vineyards is entirely organic.

From the very first bottle of Pardelasses, crafted in 2005, to the present day, the winery remains the result of a deep ambition and the beginning of a dream—something they are determined never to forget, and which continues to shape their past, present, and future.
