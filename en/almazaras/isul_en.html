ISUL | Almazara Ecológica de La Rioja

ISUL, an organic extra virgin olive oil (EVOO), is the result of a carefully preserved family tradition rooted in the heart of El Cascajo, an olive grove set within a calcareous, shallow-soiled agricultural estate located between Mount Yerga and the River Ebro, in Alfaro (La Rioja). This privileged setting lends the oil a unique character, shaped by the climate, the land, and inherited expertise.

ISUL is a prestigious, genuine oil that embodies all the health and culinary virtues of the finest extra virgin olive oils. Its quality is no accident, but the outcome of a cultivation philosophy that prioritises consumer health and respect for the environment.

In the ISUL olive grove, organic farming methods are applied. Although these reduce the yield in kilos of olives per tree and entail higher production costs, they ensure a fruit completely free from harmful residues. This commitment to organic farming not only safeguards the health of the consumer but also protects the natural balance of the environment—preventing contamination of groundwater, air, and soil layers by nitrates and other hazardous substances.
