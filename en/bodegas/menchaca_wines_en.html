MENCHACA WINES

Menchaca Wines makes original red wines from multi-varietal plots with centenary vines, cultivated in an old vineyard recovered in 1947. Located in the historic wine-growing region of El Molar, very close to Madrid, the winery gives life to wines with their own character, which are a reflection of a land rich in tradition. Among its most outstanding creations are ‘100 Palos’ and the singular wines of the ‘Botargas’ collection, the result of the effort and dedication to keep alive the essence of the traditional vineyard.

Guided by a philosophy that values authenticity and respect for history, Menchaca Wines is committed to preserving both old vineyards and traditional winemaking methods. This way of understanding wine allows us to offer unique products that transmit the soul of the land where they are born. More than a wine, each bottle is a cultural and social expression of a legacy that continues with enthusiasm generation after generation.
