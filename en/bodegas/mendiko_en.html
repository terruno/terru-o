BODEGA MENDIKO

Bodega Mendiko is a family business founded in 2001 in Aibar-Oibar, in the Lower Mountain region of Navarre. With 14 hectares of its own vineyards spread over nine estates, it grows varieties such as Garnacha, Cabernet Sauvignon, Tempranillo and Syrah using organic methods. Its artisanal and organic production is protected by the DO Navarra and has a capacity of 100,000 litres. Mendiko wines are the result of careful traditional viticulture, with an annual harvest of between 55,000 and 60,000 kilos, which is transformed into clean, bright and fruity wines, full of intense aromas of wild fruits.

The winery is the result of Victorino Ibero's commitment to the wine-growing heritage of his ancestors. Since he was a child, he learned in the family vineyards the secrets of a viticulture that respects the land, free of chemical products and based on the manual and constant care of the plant. To honour this legacy, he undertook the creation of a truly organic wine, starting with the recovery of land in Aibar-Oibar. There he planted new vines following totally natural processes, guaranteeing that neither the soil, nor the grapes, nor the wine have been in contact with pesticides, synthetic fertilisers or genetic manipulations.
