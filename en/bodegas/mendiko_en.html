BODEGA MENDIKO

Bodega Mendiko is a family-run winery founded in 2001, producing organic wines under the DO Navarra designation. The wines are crafted from 14 hectares of their own vineyards, located in the Baja Montaña region, in the Aibar Valley. The estate is organically farmed, cultivating Garnacha, Cabernet Sauvignon, Tempranillo, and Syrah.

Each sip of wine made by Bodegas Mendiko from the organically grown, artisanally crafted grapes of Aibar-Oibar carries the spirit to other times and places—to moments and landscapes when this land, the Baja Montaña, was the very heart of the Kingdom of Navarre.
