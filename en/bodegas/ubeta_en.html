UBETA | Garnacha de Monte

Ubeta is a family-run winery with deep roots in Navarra, where the winemaking tradition has been passed down from generation to generation since the early 20th century.

Located in Ablitas, this winery was born from the dream of preserving and restoring old vineyards of century-old Garnacha vines planted by their grandparents, blending the best of the forgotten and the cutting-edge. Each bottle of Ubeta reflects the region’s microclimate, soil, and wine culture, offering organic wines that stir emotions and tell the living story of a family passionate about viticulture and the art of winemaking.

At Ubeta, the vineyard is seen as a sacred heritage to be cared for by future generations. Their approach to wine remains rooted in the same vision of friendship and enjoyment held by their ancestors: a pleasure to share and celebrate.

Ubeta creates unique wines that not only capture the essence of Navarra’s Garnacha, but also pay tribute to the family legacy and the profound bond between the land, the vine, and those who tend it.
