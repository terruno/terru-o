BODEGAS EMILIO VALERIO | Laderas de Montejurra

The Emilio Valerio winery produces its wines with a deep respect for the land and the natural rhythms of the vineyard. They practice organic and biodynamic viticulture, without the use of chemical products, and cultivate each plot using manual and traditional methods, including tillage with horses. This way of understanding the countryside, in harmony with the environment, gives rise to authentic and expressive wines, which reflect the character of the landscape from which they come. All their wines are certified organic and biodynamic, and have been recognised both nationally and internationally for their quality and uniqueness.

With a winemaking tradition that dates back to the 14th century, the wineries have been able to preserve the family legacy by recovering ancestral techniques in vineyard work and winemaking. They have also led the way in the recovery of native Navarrese varieties, such as Garnacha, and maintain vineyards that are over a hundred years old. This combination of history, territorial identity and commitment to sustainability results in wines with their own personality, deeply rooted in their origins.
