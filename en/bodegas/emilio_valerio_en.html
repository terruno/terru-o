BODEGAS EMILIO VALERIO | Laderas de Montejurra

Emilio Valerio - Laderas de Montejurra is a family-run winery distinguished by its commitment to preserving the biodiversity of the Montejurra valleys, practising biodynamic agriculture, and producing high-quality wines on the southern slopes of Montejurra, across more than 50 small plots located in the Tierra Estella sub-region.

The vineyard is cultivated using traditional, artisanal methods with minimal mechanisation. Harvesting is done by hand, and no pesticides or herbicides have been used for over fifteen years. Instead, biodynamic treatments are applied. In the winery, the approach is deliberately simple: each vineyard is vinified separately in concrete vats or wooden tuns, and the wines are aged exclusively in French oak, with only a very limited use of new barrels.
