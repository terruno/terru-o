ALBET i NOYA

Albet i Noya is a family-owned winery located in the Ordal Mountains, in the Penedès region, and has been a pioneer in organic winemaking since 1978. From the very beginning, they have worked with a strong environmental commitment, cultivating their vineyards without pesticides or herbicides and embracing sustainability as the path forward. Their estate, Can Vendrell de la Codina, stretches across Mediterranean forests and dry-stone terraces, in a biodiverse landscape that gives character to their wines.

With a history dating back to 1903 and now in the hands of the fifth generation, Albet i Noya combines a long winemaking tradition with continuous innovation. They were the first in Catalonia to research resistant grape varieties (PIWI), and today their organic wines are recognized for both their quality and authenticity — a true reflection of the landscape they come from.
