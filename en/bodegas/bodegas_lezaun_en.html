BODEGAS LEZAUN

Lezaun Winery is a family-run estate located in Lakar, a privileged village in Navarra nestled between the Urbasa and Andia mountain ranges. This unique setting, with a climate that blends Atlantic and Mediterranean influences, provides ideal conditions for organic viticulture. Since its beginnings in 1780, the Lezaun family has passed down their passion and winemaking knowledge through generations, keeping tradition alive while adapting to modern demands.

The winery stands out as one of Navarra’s pioneers in producing organic wines, always committed to sustainable farming without the use of herbicides, pesticides, or synthetic chemicals.

Throughout its history, Lezaun Winery has skillfully balanced tradition and innovation. From reviving ancestral winemaking methods in the 1980s to opening a grill house in the 1990s, they have created a space where wine culture meets gastronomy and leisure. Today, their organic wines compete internationally and have earned numerous awards, underscoring the winery’s dedication to quality and sustainable development.
