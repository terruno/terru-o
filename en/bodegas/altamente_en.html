ALTAMENTE

Altamente is a family project born from a passion for Monastrell and a commitment to preserving its essence in every bottle. Located in a valley at 900 meters above sea level, the winery cultivates organic grapes on own-rooted vines in shallow limestone soils, under a mountain climate influenced by the Mediterranean. These unique conditions allow the creation of elegant, balanced, and fruity wines that truly reflect the character of this Mediterranean variety.

At the heart of the Jumilla DO, Altamente works with vineyards that blend tradition and environmental respect. Monastrell, a resilient and distinctive grape, is grown here without grafting, keeping alive an ancient practice that adds authenticity and complexity to the wines. Each cluster brings intensity and freshness, the result of meticulous care during cultivation and harvest.

The winery, recently built, is designed for the artisanal and controlled production of high-quality wines. Nestled among the vineyards themselves, it allows Altamente to maintain a close connection to the land and oversee every step of the process. In this way, the winery offers wines that are much more than a drink: they are a pure expression of their terroir and the family passion behind them.
