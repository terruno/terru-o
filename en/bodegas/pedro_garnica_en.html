Bodegas PEDRO GARNICA

Bodegas Pedro Garnica produces wines with authenticity and character, born from a deep respect for the land and a strong family legacy. Their vineyards are located on the upper slopes of the northeastern Sierra de Yerga, in the region of La Rioja Baja, at altitudes ranging between 570 and 690 meters. This privileged setting, with its extreme climate and poor soils, offers ideal conditions for growing traditional grape varieties such as Tempranillo, Garnacha, and Graciano.

Today, the vines still show their twisted, timeworn shapes, living proof of decades of adaptation and history. In some plots, you can still find scattered ancient varieties that once shaped the local viticultural landscape, such as Maturana Tinta. At Bodegas Pedro Garnica, each vintage is approached with a philosophy of minimal intervention, always aiming to capture the uniqueness of the vineyard and the true character of every harvest. Their deep commitment to rural life and sustainable practices makes this winery a genuine example of rooted, forward-looking viticulture.
