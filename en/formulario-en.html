About Us

Terruños Únicos is born of love and passion for the land, for the authenticity and personality of what grows from its roots without betraying it and from the senses that connect us to what is essential.

Our passion became our mission: to share new culinary and sensory experiences — in short, lived experiences.

During our search, which has been going on for more than 25 years now, we have found and continue to discover jewels, precious stones, little unknown treasures that never cease to surprise us.

Far from the mainstream —the dominant, commercial path— are these producers who do not give in to sales arguments or compromises that do not coincide with their own principles.

We try to understand their mentality, their culture, their land, and their personal character... Their unique terroir!

Which, in the end, results in something valuable we can share with you: those of us who are looking for the authentic, the surprising, the less known and the most typical that is not so easy to find!

We’ll try to send it directly to your home. If not by our local point of distribution? Then we make our own ‘Logistic-Management’ to get it ASAP to your destiny.

We're here to help: reach out anytime, no obligation.
