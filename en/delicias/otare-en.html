OTARE

Otare is a brand by LAKET, a distributor based in Estella (Navarre), that brings together a carefully curated selection of raw materials from local producers in the region.

Their products stand out for their artisanal production methods and minimal processing, ensuring the highest quality and attention to detail. Otare is also committed to organic farming, respecting the environment and offering genuine, natural foods.
