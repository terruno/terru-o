GURE EZTIA

Since 1993, GURE EZTIA has brought together a group of Basque beekeepers committed to working in harmony with nature and with their bees. Their goal is simple: to offer raw, fresh, and handcrafted honey of the highest purity and quality, proudly bearing the Eusko Label.

Their honey is never pasteurized or exposed to high heat, which helps preserve all of its natural properties. It’s produced in small batches, with care and cold-packed to maintain its signature creamy texture and remarkable freshness you can taste from the very first spoonful.

Choosing GURE EZTIA means more than enjoying exceptional honey, it means supporting a local project that protects the environment and promotes biodiversity.
