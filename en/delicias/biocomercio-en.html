BIOcomercio

BIOcomercio is a project dedicated to promoting organic production from Alicante. They work with both organic raw materials intended for processing and packaging, as well as ready-to-consume organic products.

Their commitment is to use Spanish raw materials and, whenever possible, sourced from nearby areas, thus encouraging local farming and supporting the regional economy. Additionally, they run their own plantation projects in Alicante, actively contributing to the growth of organic agriculture.

At BIOcomercio, they ensure food safety, quality, and traceability at every stage — from the fieldwork to the product reaching the consumer. Their goal is to offer authentic, healthy, and environmentally friendly products designed for those who value natural, locally sourced production.
